My Approach

A HUMAN-CENTERED DESIGN

Visual design might catch user attention, but human-focused design is what keeps users coming back. Clean, thoughtful interfaces help users feel at ease, yet to influence a user's behaviour and engagement within your product, one has to look at creating an experience that resonates with the user.

My approach is rooted in human-focused design first, understanding real desires and applying principles from behavioural economics to create visuals that speak to human psychology. This helps shape user decisions in ways that improve experience and drive measurable outcomes — like increased engagement, conversion, and retention.
